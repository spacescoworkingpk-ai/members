A coworking space in Federal B Area, in the middle of Karachi.

Spaces is a managed coworking space and shared office on the Mezzanine Floor, C-10, Block 4, Federal B Area, Karachi. If you have been searching for a desk or an office near you in Karachi Central, you are probably closer than you think.

Where we are, and who we are near

Our address is Mezzanine Floor, C-10, Block 4, Federal B Area, Karachi, with street parking outside and the Brue & Spaces sign at the entrance. Because Federal B Area sits in the centre of the city, we are a short drive from several of Karachi's busiest neighbourhoods:

  • Gulberg Town
  • North Nazimabad and Nazimabad
  • Liaquatabad
  • Gulshan-e-Iqbal
  • The wider Karachi Central district

What Spaces offers

Whether you work alone or lead a team, there is a way to work here that fits. Flexible hot desks start at Rs 15,500 a month, dedicated desks with a locker are Rs 17,500, and furnished private rooms for teams start at Rs 18,500 per person. Visiting for less time? A day pass is Rs 1,500 and a weekly pass is Rs 5,000.

  • Full pricing for desks, rooms and passes
  • Private offices for teams of four to eleven
  • Day passes with no commitment
  • A meeting room by the hour

Open day and night

The morning shift runs Monday to Friday 9:00am–6:30pm and Saturday 9:00am–4:30pm. There is also a night shift, 9:00pm–6:00am Monday to Friday, on the Flexible Desk and Dedicated Plus plans — useful if your hours do not fit the usual working day. The whole floor runs on backup power, so load shedding does not stop your work.
