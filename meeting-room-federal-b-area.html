A meeting and conference room in Federal B Area, Karachi.

When a call becomes a proper conversation — a client pitch, an interview, a team planning session — you need a real room, not a café corner. Book our meeting room by the hour, with a proper table, a screen and the quiet to get it done.

Booking and price

The meeting room is Rs 1,500 an hour. Members on the Dedicated Desk, Personal Desk and Dedicated Plus plans get 12 conference-room hours a month included. Walk-ins and non-members are welcome too — message us on WhatsApp to check availability and reserve a slot.

What the room is set up for

  • A proper conference table with seating for a small group
  • A screen for presentations and video calls
  • High-speed Wi-Fi and backup power
  • A quiet, private space away from the shared floor
  • Reception support and refreshments on request

Good for

Client meetings, job interviews, team stand-ups, investor pitches, training sessions and video calls that need a clean, quiet background. Because we are in Federal B Area, it is an easy, central place for people coming from Gulberg, North Nazimabad, Nazimabad and across Karachi Central to gather.
