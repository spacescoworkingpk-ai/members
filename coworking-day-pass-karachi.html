How a day pass works

There is nothing to sign and no membership to buy. Message us on WhatsApp or simply come to the Mezzanine Floor, C-10, Block 4, Federal B Area. Pay for the day, take any open desk in the shared workspace, and use the Wi-Fi, backup power, cafeteria and reception for as long as you are here.

  • Any open desk in the shared workspace
  • High-speed Wi-Fi and backup power
  • Complimentary tea and coffee, plus Brue Coffee at reception
  • Access to the lobby and dining area
  • Printing and photocopying at the front desk

When a day pass makes sense

Day passes suit visitors to Karachi, remote workers between meetings, students needing a quiet place to focus, and anyone trying Spaces before committing to a monthly desk. If you find yourself coming back, a Flexible Desk membership is Rs 15,500 a month and works out cheaper than ten day passes.
